## ğŸ’¡ Learning Strategies & Tips

### Spaced Repetition

- Use Anki for vocabulary
- Review words at intervals: 1 day, 3 days, 1 week, 2 weeks, 1 month
- Focus on recall, not recognition

### Immersion Tactics

- Change phone/computer language to French
- Label items in your home with French words
- Think in French (internal monologue)
- Consume only French content for 1-2 hours daily

### Active Learning

- Don't just watch/listenâ€”engage
- Pause videos and repeat
- Write down new expressions
- Use new words in sentences immediately

### Consistency Over Intensity

> **30 minutes daily > 3.5 hours once a week**
- Build habits, not motivation
- Track streaks

### Learn Phrases, Not Just Words

- "Comment allez-vous?" instead of just "comment"
- "Ã‡a me plaÃ®t" instead of just "plaÃ®t"
- Chunks are easier to remember and use
